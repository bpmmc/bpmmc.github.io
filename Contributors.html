New York . Electronic / Alternative . 1998 . Discover the artist

Anna Massek

I’m a singer-songwriter/producer based out of NYC, where I classically trained my voice at the Manhattan School of Music. I began writing original pieces on guitar at 13 and started producing electronic music three years later. Ellie Goulding, Daughter and Banks are my main influences; listening to them while growing up has led me to create a sound that is heavily grounded in downtempo electronic music, yet also incorporates acoustic elements. I’m currently studying at the University of St Andrews alongside performing and djing in and around the town.

J. Quow

As an artist my music is all about my past, present, and future. Its more than the stories and snapshots for me - it's the driving force. I live on the impact of being both a producer and lyricist. The process of creating a soundscape or beat and adding intelligible life to it through rhythmic and melodic word. That's artistry is for me - being both the architect and the mason.

Don't Walk

In the wake of the 9/11 attacks in 2001, a multinational group of St Andrews students, 3000 miles from the epicentre, decided to respond. DONT WALK was born, a symbolic response in essence, its spirit youthful and cosmopolitan. Through various artistic mediums, we communicate our controversial and unorthodox vision. It is this unique voice and agency of our team that makes this, and every year's iteration of DONT WALK such a celebration of the creative spirit.
